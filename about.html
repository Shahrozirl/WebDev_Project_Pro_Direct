About Snow Trails

Welcome to Snow Trails, your ultimate destination for trusted, snow locations. Our mission is simple but vital: to raise the standard of satisfaction. Whether you're a new experiencer or a seasoned enthusiast, Snow Trails is your go-to website.

The Alps (+ all the other mountain ranges) are some of the most captivating (and dangerous) places on Earth. From the elegence of Mount Everest to Carountoohill in Ireland, each nature spot should be visited. Unfortunately, it can be difficult to get to certain locations. That's where we come in — to cut through the myths and provide excellent service and help.

Every care guide, tip, and travel recommendation is carefully compiled by experienced hoobyists, agencies, and even the locals! We don't just tell you what to do — we explain the why behind it, a proper story to tell once you get back!

At Snow Trails, we believe that sustainability is more than a concept — it's a responsibility. We encourage everyone to stay mindful of the enviroment when visiting thses exotic places. Treat it like your onw home!

Our website includes detailed species-specific care guides, shopping lists, equipment setup instructions, and much more( — we’ve got you covered)

Behind Snow Trails is a passionate team. We are hobbyists, researchers, educators, and above all, snow sport lovers. Our goal is to build a global community of responsible enthusiasts who value the importance of your nature.
