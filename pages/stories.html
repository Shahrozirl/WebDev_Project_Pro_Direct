Snow Trails - Stories

Mila booked her first winter escape with SnowTrails and spent the weekend carving through powdery slopes she’d only seen in photos. By sunset, she was sipping hot cocoa in a mountaintop lodge, already planning her next trip.

When the Patel family joined a SnowTrails group tour, they expected simple skiing lessons—what they got was a week of laughter, snowball fights, and unforgettable memories shared with new friends from all over the world.

Elias, a photographer, traveled with SnowTrails to capture the northern peaks. Instead of just photos, he returned home with stories of sunrise hikes, frozen lakes, and a sense of peace he hadn’t felt in years.

For her birthday, Tessa surprised her friends with a SnowTrails adventure. They spent the day snowshoeing through quiet forests and the evening celebrating around a crackling fire under the stars.

After a long year, Jamal needed a break. SnowTrails took him to a cozy alpine village where he rediscovered the joy of slow mornings, fresh snowfalls, and exploring hidden trails with a friendly guide.

Sofia and Leo joined a SnowTrails weekend retreat hoping for a simple getaway. Between exhilarating downhill runs and peaceful gondola rides, they found a shared love for winter travel they’d never known before.
